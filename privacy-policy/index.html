Our Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy

We use Cloudflare to protect our site. We have analytics which records the number of unique IP addresses to calculate number of visitors.

We collect

  1. IP ADDRESS We do not directly store your IP Addresses (Unless you are a bot). We calculate the Approximate location based on country to view our stats
  2. The aggregate data of response given by the server. I.e., Whether site is served or is it not. Whether the site used TLS and which type of TLS did the server use.
  3. We keep the record of the number of people who visited our site. This is shown by Cloudflare. So, the aggregate data is stored up there and the aggregate data is stored for a month in Cloudflare servers
  4. In case that you are suspected as a bot, your IP address is recorded alongside with the URL and the data of the browser used that you have tried to fetch.
  5. Do not worry about your privacy unless you are a robot. Only robots and its owners need to worry about the data that is recorded. A legitimate user’s data is very rarely recorded.
  6. We do not disclose the data that is logged in the Cloudflare servers.

When is your exact IP visible to us?

  1. When you have visited our site and tried to access the folders which are forbidden. When you try to access, then you are blocked from accessing that page and your IP address is blocked.
  2. When you are highly suspicious, and a JavaScript or Captcha challenge is shown to you.

Please note that all your data is logged by Cloudflare and not Swamy and Thomas.
These all are for your good and our safety of website

Refer Cloudflare Privacy policy for end users for detailed Insights.
