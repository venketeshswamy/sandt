About Swamy and Thomas

Swamy and Thomas is a partnership firm which commenced its operations in the year 1996. We are well known by our former name “Formation House”. In the year 2017, we changed our name from Formation House to Swamy and Thomas

Let it be core construction or exterior or even interiors, we are ready to help you. Just leave the project on us and we would look up the rest.

Vision of Swamy and Thomas

In Swamy and Thomas, Sustainability of the projects, client satisfaction paired with aesthetics and longevity is the core mission.

Sustainability

Let us leave resources for our future generations.
In Swamy and Thomas, sustainability has the foremost priority.
We focus to reduce impact of construction on the environment

Client Satisfaction

In Swamy & Thomas, Client satisfaction is next to sustainability . We try to get all the suggestions from clients and try the best to implement them.

We notify the changes that we find good for longevity and aesthetics of the project done.

Swamy and Thomas also tries to reduce the cost of project without compromising in the quality of materials.

The story behind Swamy and Thomas

In Mid of February of 1996, Hybeen Thomas and Venkatesan left their job as project managers of Sterling Holiday Resorts. They wanted to be employers than employees.

The partners started a company called “Queensland Constructions & Properties Pvt Ltd” in the same year. The company was in operations until 1999.

Then, Formation House commenced its operations in the year 2002 and was operational until 2017. We planned to drop our construction and shift to hospitality.

But, many clients who wanted us to continue the construction. So, the Partnership S & T was incorporated with the same partners as before in the dawn of 2017.
