About Us

About Swamy and Thomas

Ours is a partnership firm which commenced its operations in the year 1996. We are well known by our former name “Formation House”. In the year 2017 we changed our name from Formation House to Swamy and Thomas

Let it be exteriors or interiors or core construction, we are ready to help. Just leave it on us and we would look up the rest.
We have done various projects especially Hotels and Resorts and hence we have an edge in construction of hospitality assets.

Our Vision

In Swamy and Thomas, Sustainability of the projects, client satisfaction paired with aesthetics and longevity is the core mission.

Sustainability

Client Satisfaction

In Swamy & Thomas, Client satisfaction is next to sustainability . We try to get all the suggestions from clients and try the best to implement them.

Our Story

It was the year 1996,

In Mid of February, Hybeen Thomas and Venkatesan who were working as project managers (construction) of Sterling Hotels and Resorts Munnar left the job in scope of being self-reliant by employing people rather than being employees.

We started a company called “Queensland Constructions & Properties Pvt Ltd” in the same year. The company was in operations until 1999.

Then, Formation House commenced it operations in the year 2002 and was operational until 2017. We planned to drop our construction and shift to hospitality.

But, there were clients who wanted us to continue the construction. So, the Partnership S & T was incorporated with the same partners as before in the dawn of 2017.
