Blackberry Hills Retreat, Munnar

About

Podamedu, Munnar

Client- Sahya Savari Holidays PVT LTD, Thrissur

Project Completion Year- 2004

Architect: Shahid Associates, Calicut

Site: www.blackberryresorts.com

Blackberry hills’ construction commenced in the year 2002. It has 16 rooms spread over an area of about 8 Acres.

The property was assisted by Ar. Rajesh George. The centerpiece is the restaurant (Named Hornbill) which is made of Terracotta tiles and roofing. We are sure that you would enjoy the experience there.

It is one of the most loved resorts in Munnar. We would like you to browse their site if you need to know more information about the property. If you are keen in seeing the photos of the property, please google “Blackberry Hills Munnar”.

We are sorry that since it has been a long that we have constructed the resort, we do not have much photos in our systems.
